Description

A completo is a chilean style hotdog with avocado and tomato.

Ingredients:

  • 4 hot dogs
  • 1 large ripe avocado, halved and pitted
  • 2 teaspoons hot sauce (such as Cholula)
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic (about 1 clove), mashed to a paste
  • ½ teaspoon fresh lime juice (from 1 lime)
  • ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 4 hot dog buns
  • ½ cup prepared sauerkraut
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Cook hot dogs according to package directions.
  2. Mash avocado until mostly smooth. Stir in hot sauce, garlic, lime juice, and salt. Place hot dogs in buns and top each with 3 tablespoons avocado mixture, 2 tablespoons sauerkraut, and 1 tablespoon mayonnaise. Serve immediately with lime wedges.
